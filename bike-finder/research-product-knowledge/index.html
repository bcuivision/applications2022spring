Summary

For a potential buyer, the most critical factors that they will be faced with are purpose, budget, size, and style. The reason for purchasing determines bike types, and the budget narrows down available products by price. Last, personal preferences like color and size decide what bike may be best for the buyer.

In my personal opinion, a potential online buyer’s priorities should be purpose and budget.

Patterns & Anti-Patterns

  • Filter
  • A filter feature seems crucial for a bike store, as there is an overwhelming amount of information.
  • Therefore, an efficient way to organize all the information is critical.
  • The ability to go back to the previous page
  • While exploring the products, I wished that the site provided a preview feature to avoid the user going back and forth in the content architecture.
  • Sign up pop up
  • Unexpected pop-ups on some sites felt invasive.
